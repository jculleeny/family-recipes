Bacon Pasta

  • 3 slices of bacon
  • 1/2 box of preferred straight pasta
  • olive oil

Origin: New York Times

I stayed with an italian room mate for a couple of weeks, and he made a version of this for us, and it was delicious. I never thought to ask what it was called before he left. After some research I found it, and made it for Bri and I while she was pregnant with Jack, and it was amazing. It has been a favorite of ours for a simple meal ever since.





Instructions

Step 1

Put a pan on medium low with plenty of olive oil.

Alternatives and Additions

  • Try adding roasted or parboiled broccoli.
